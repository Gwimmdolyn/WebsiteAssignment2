Adoption Process

Your guide to adopting a new pet

Step-by-Step Adoption Process

  1. Search for a pet using the filters on the home page.
  2. Save your favourite pets and create a user profile.
  3. Book an appointment to visit the shelter and meet the pet in person.
  4. Complete the adoption application form at the shelter.
  5. Bring your new pet home and provide a loving environment.

For more details, contact your local shelter.

Adoption Requirements

We are excited to help you find your perfect pet! Before you adopt, please ensure you meet the following requirements:

  • Be at least 18 years of age or older.
  • Have a valid government-issued ID.
  • Provide proof of residence (rental agreement or ownership document).
  • Demonstrate financial ability to care for a pet (proof of income or savings).
  • Commit to providing regular veterinary care, proper training, and a loving home.

If you have any questions about these requirements, please contact us.
